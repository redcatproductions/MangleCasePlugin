Mangle Case Plugin for Eclipse

Requirements:

Eclipse 3.3.1.1 or greater (tested with 3.3.1.1, 3.3.2 and 3.4) running on Java SE 5 or greater

Installation:

  • Please navigate to the BMW Group - System AU - Eclipse update site at http://ltintra.muc:5022/eclipse

Usage:

The plugin offers convenient generation of resource bundle files during development or build processes of the LCC-Workspace and AU-RAIN.
